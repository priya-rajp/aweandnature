About the Study

This study aims to understand the feeling of awe when images of nature are observed.

The first questionnaire takes 2-5 minutes to complete and is completely voluntary.

The following surveys take 5-7 minutes each for 13 consecutive days.

Eligibility Criteria: Anyone above 18 years old and available daily for 2 weeks continuously in the evenings (GMT).
